About Data Sources

Data sources represent external entities that provide water quality data to your system. They can be:

  • Labs: Testing laboratories that analyze water samples
  • Research Institutions: Universities or research centers (e.g., IIT Madras)
  • Government Agencies: Official government water quality monitoring programs
  • NGOs: Non-governmental organizations conducting water testing
  • WHO GLAAS: World Health Organization Global Analysis and Assessment data

Note: Data sources with existing imports cannot be deleted. You must first remove all associated import batches.
