About Jal Sarovar

Water Quality Management System

Jal Sarovar is a comprehensive water quality management system designed for the Amrit Sarovar Mission under the Jal Jeevan initiative.

The system combines traditional laboratory testing with advanced Machine Learning models to provide accurate contamination detection, risk assessment, and predictive analytics for water bodies across India.

ML Models
  • Site Risk Classifier - Random Forest model for risk level prediction
  • Contamination Classifier - XGBoost model for contamination type detection
  • WQI Calculator - Real-time Water Quality Index calculation
  • Anomaly Detector - Isolation Forest for anomaly detection
  • Quality Forecaster - Gaussian Process for water quality forecasting
  • Cost Optimizer - Bayesian optimization for testing schedule optimization
